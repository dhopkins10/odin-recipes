Vegetable Fried Rice Recipe

Description

This recipe can be made for lunch or dinner as a side or as its own entrée! Perfect for meat-eaters and vegetarians alike!

Ingredients

  • 1/2 tbsp unsalted butter
  • Small white onion
  • 3 eggs whisked
  • 2 cups frozen peas and carrots
  • 1/2 tsp minced onion
  • 1 tbsp minced garlic
  • 2 1/2 cups cooked white rice
  • 4-5 tbsp soy sauce
  • 2-3 tsp sesame oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Melt butter in a large skillet over medium heat. Add eggs and cook until scrambled. Remove to plate.
  2. In the same skillet, add frozen peas and carrots, and minced onion. Saute for 5 minutes or until tender. Add minced garlic and sauté for about 30 seconds.
  3. Add rice and soy sauce to skillet, stirring to combine. Be sure to taste rice with soy sauce, as you may need to increase/decrease amount, depending on your preference.
  4. Cook for 7-10 minutes, stirring occasionally, in order to fry rice.
  5. Turn heat to low, add eggs and sesame oil. Stir to combine.
  6. Remove from heat. Serve immediately or refrigerate until ready to serve.
