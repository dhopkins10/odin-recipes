French Toast

Description

This rich, classic french toast recipe is easy to make and only requires a few ingredients that you most likely already have in your kitchen. Any bread will do!

Ingredients

  • 6 large eggs
  • 1 1/2 cups heavy cream or half & half
  • 2 tbsp pure vanilla extract
  • 1/2 tsp ground cinnamon
  • pinch of ground nutmeg
  • pinch of salt
  • 6 slices of (1-inch-thick) bread, preferably stale
  • 4 tbsp unsalted butter
  • 4 tbsp vegetable oil
